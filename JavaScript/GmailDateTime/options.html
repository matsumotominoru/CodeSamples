Date/time format: Default: (%Y-%m-%d %H:%M)
Currently supported format specifiers:
  • %Y - Year (2017)
  • %y - Year (00-99)
  • %m - Month (01-12)
  • %-m - Month (1-12)
  • %b - Abbreviated month name (Mar)
  • %B - Full month name (March)
  • %U - Week number
  • %d - Day of the month (01-31)
  • %-d - Day of the month (1-31)
  • %a - Abbreviated weekday name (Wed)
  • %A - Full weekday name (Wednesday)
  • %H - Hour (00-23)
  • %-H - Hour (0-23)
  • %I - Hour (01-12)
  • %-I - Hour (1-12)
  • %M - Minute (00-59)
  • %p - AM/PM
More formatting options may be added on request.
